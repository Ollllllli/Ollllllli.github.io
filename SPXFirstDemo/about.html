About

Minecraft is a sandbox adventure based game made in the autumn of 2009.

It was made by a Swedish developer known as Notch, who quickly made a name for himself and built up a team of people to work with him.

The game is set in a randomly generated, near infinite world, that you can explore, fight monsters in, search for diamonds to make the best armour, and ultimately defeat the end game boss, the Ender Dragon.

Originally, Notch(and his fellow dev, Jeb) and his team owned Minecraft, under the company; Mojang. However, a few years ago Microsoft bought ownership of Mojang.
